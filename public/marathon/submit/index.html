How can I get a link to my profile? 🤔

From phone:

  1. Open the Facebook mobile app.
  2. Go to the “Home” tab in the bottom menu.
  3. Tap the three dots button under your name.
  4. Scroll all the way down.
  5. Tap “Share profile.”
  6. Tap “Copy Link.”
  7. Enter the copied link in the area above.

From PC:

  1. Go to your Facebook profile on any browser.
  2. Copy and paste the URL from your profile page.
    The link should look like this:
    https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=77777777777777
